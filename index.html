I am a second year PhD student in Computational Linguistics at the University of Cambridge. I am a member of the Language Technology Lab (LTL), working on computational models of lexical representation. My supervisors are Professor Anna Korhonen, and Dr. Diana Mccarthy. I am a member of Peterhouse College and I am funded by Peterhouse Research Studentship.
